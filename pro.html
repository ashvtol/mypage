1. Online Journal

A secret diary or a private journal of ideas, notes and pictures that could be accessible from anywhere. The main motivation was secrecy, maintaining a diary in the hostel was tough because friends visited the rooms day in and day out and in such a scenario maintaining a secret diary was almost impossible.So we made an online journal where in every user would get a private space toexpress their feelings freely. We introduced themes so that users could add pictures, personalize their private space according to their moods and very importantly we encrypted their data. This project was based on Database Management System

2. Codenigma

Codenigma was an online game where students had to solve multiple choice questions and programming problems within a stipulated time. The game was part of annual event of NIT Rourkela's Microsoft Campus Club. Various Technologies like, PHP, HTML, CSS, SQL was used. The overall experience through the process was very fruitful and enjoyable.
